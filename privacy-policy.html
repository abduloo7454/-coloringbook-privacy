Privacy Policy

My Coloring Book · Last updated: June 12, 2026

My Coloring Book is a simple coloring application designed for children. This policy explains our approach to privacy in plain language.

Information We Collect

We collect no information of any kind. The app does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information, usage data, device identifiers, or analytics. There are no user accounts, no registration, and no forms.

Internet Access

The app works entirely offline. It makes no network connections, contains no links to external websites, and does not communicate with any server.

Advertising and Purchases

The app contains no advertising of any kind and no in-app purchases.

Children's Privacy

The app is designed for children and complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA), the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), and Google Play Families and Apple Kids Category policies. Because the app collects no data whatsoever, no personal information from children or any other user is ever processed.

Third-Party Services

The app uses no third-party services, SDKs, or analytics tools.

Changes to This Policy

If the app ever changes in a way that affects privacy, this policy will be updated and the date above revised before any such version is released.
